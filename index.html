How to Use Fisho

Take or Select a Photo

Use the camera to take a fresh photo of your fish, or choose an existing photo from your library.

Position Your Fish

Pinch to zoom and drag to position the fish perfectly in the frame for better identification.

Analyze

Tap "Analyze Fish" and our AI will identify the species and estimate size in seconds.

Share or Correct

Share your results or help improve our AI by correcting any predictions.

Photo Tips for Better Results

  • Good lighting: Natural daylight works best
  • Clear view: Show the entire fish without obstructions
  • Side angle: Profile shots work better than top-down views
  • Close-up: Fill the frame with the fish for detailed analysis
  • Still fish: Photos work better than videos
